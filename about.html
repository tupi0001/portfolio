About me

My name is Daria, and I'm a budding photographer who is truly passionate about creativity and pushing the boundaries through conceptual photography. I love experimenting with different ideas and transforming them into visually captivating images. Whether it's commercial shoots or personal projects, I pour my heart and soul into each photograph, always seeking to capture the essence of a moment or emotion. Photography isn't just a job for me; it's my hobby and a way to express myself and explore the world around me through the lens of my camera.

APPROACH TO PHOTOGRAPHY

We don't just capture moments; we craft stories through the lens. Our approach to photography goes beyond focusing on immersive narratives and captivating visuals.

STUDIO BACKGROUND

Minimalism, geometric shapes, designer furniture with bright accents – these are the elements combined in the Swan hall. A bright location for shooting with light satin white curtains and plenty of natural light, making it easy and convenient to create voluminous portraits and model tests.
